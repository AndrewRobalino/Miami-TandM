# Miami Taxes & Management Co.

A modern, interactive marketing website built to support a local Miami-based family business — helping them establish a professional online presence and connect with their community.

## About

Miami Taxes & Management Co. is a father and son tax preparation business with over 20 years of experience serving individuals and families across Miami. This site was built to give them a polished, client-facing platform that reflects the trust and professionalism they bring to every client.

## Built With

- **React + Vite** — fast, component-based frontend
- **Framer Motion** — smooth page transitions and animations
- **Tailwind CSS** — clean, responsive styling

## Features

- Animated landing page with interactive tax form visuals
- Smooth section-based navigation (Services, Contact, About Us)
- Fully responsive layout
- Contact integration with WhatsApp and email
